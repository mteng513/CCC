Cornell Consulting Club

Education ※ Hands-on experience ※ Networking

CCC is the leading and oldest undergraduate consulting organization at Cornell. For our clients we offer consulting services, solving business problems in functional areas such as strategy, management, business development, operations, marketing, finance and technology. For our members, we offer a truly unique hands-on experience where our consultants get to work in teams and solve challenging business problems for clients in industries including high-tech, financial services, venture capital, oil & gas, investment banking, non-profit, education, restaurant, government and much more. Our past engagements have included big clients such as a top-10 venture capital firm, a top-10 business school, and an international European asset manager, as well as smaller clients such as several local startups and a regional museum.

Cornerstone Research 2015 Case Competition

Join us and the team from Cornerstone Research for a case competition, information session and networking session, with free food and drinks!

Service Offerings

Our services have traditionally concentrated in sales and marketing strategy, organizational strategy and business unit strategy to companies in different industries including private equity, energy and gas, high technology and financial services.

Office Visits

CCC visits top consulting firms in NYC at the beginning of every semester, right before internship opportunities or full-time recruiting.
